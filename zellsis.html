Biografia Zellsis

Jordan "Zellsis" Montemurro (born March 2, 1998) is a retired American professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player. He retired on May 10, 2020 to pursue a career in VALORANT before returning to CS:GO a month later on June 24, 2020. [1][2] He retired again on September 27, 2020 to pursue another attempt at a career in VALORANT after joining Orgles5 alongside dazzLe. [3]
