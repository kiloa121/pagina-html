Biografia de Mixwell

Óscar "mixwell" Cañellas (born October 10, 1995) is a retired Spanish streamer who last worked for Team Heretics as a streamer. He came to VALORANT from a professional competitive Counter-Strike: Global Offensive and Counter-Strike: Source career, being best known for his time on OpTic Gaming where he won a variety of large events, with the team peaking at #2 on the global HLTV rankings.[1]
